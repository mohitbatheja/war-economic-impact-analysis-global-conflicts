# 🌍 War Economic Impact Analysis (Global Conflicts)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end **Data Analysis** of the **War Economic Impact dataset** using **Python**. The main objective is to analyze how wars and conflicts affect economic conditions across different countries and regions.

The project focuses on understanding the impact of conflicts on **GDP, unemployment, poverty, inflation, currency stability, black market activities, and reconstruction costs** through Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and data visualization.

This project demonstrates the complete Data Analytics workflow including data cleaning, preprocessing, outlier detection, exploratory analysis, and business insight generation.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze economic impact of wars across different regions
- Study unemployment changes before and during conflicts
- Identify regions with higher inflation during wars
- Analyze GDP decline and economic damage
- Compare poverty levels before and during conflicts
- Understand currency devaluation and black market impact
- Identify the most affected economic sectors
- Generate data-driven insights using visualization techniques

# 💡 Key Insights

- Wars negatively impact GDP growth and economic stability.
- Conflict periods increase unemployment rates.
- Inflation rises due to economic disruption and supply constraints.
- Poverty increases during prolonged conflicts.
- Currency depreciation increases black market activities.
- Different sectors experience different levels of economic damage.
- Reconstruction costs create long-term financial challenges.
